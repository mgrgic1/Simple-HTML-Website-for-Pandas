Our Mission

Our team's goal is to bring awareness to the ever shrinking panda population across the globe. According to researchers, only 1,864 giant pandas still remained in the wild as of 2014. This is a large increase compared to past decades, but it's still too low for our books! That's why we've set up this website in order to bring you more information about the path towards more pandas. With your helpful donations, we can support pandas everywhere and bring their numbers into the tens of thousands!
